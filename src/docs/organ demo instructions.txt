TO TURN ON:

1. Turn on the white power bar next to the iMac.

2. Open the air valves from the wall (main valve, top two yellow valves (PPC main supply), top two red valves (PPC command signals)).

3. Open organ_start.maxpat on the Desktop. If it doesnâ€™t work, quit Max completely and try again.

TO TURN OFF:

1. Close the air valves main wall. Open the third red valve (with no hose attached) to bleed the remaining air pressure.
